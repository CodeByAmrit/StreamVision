Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to StreamVision. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your information.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not store, record, or view your RTSP streams or personal credentials. Your RTSP URL is processed in-memory only to establish a temporary connection and is discarded once the stream stops.

3. Information for Customer Support

For customer support via our portal at service.cctvcameralive.in, we use Google OAuth for authentication. We only collect your email address to identify you as a customer and communicate regarding support issues.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures, including HTTPS, CSP, and Helmet, to protect our platform and any data in transit. We take security very seriously to ensure your privacy is maintained.
